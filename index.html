Technical indicators

There are a number of tools online that allow users to create charts for technical analysis - but these don't cover some of smaller, less used price series. (The above is from www.barchart.com)

Portland Wheat

  • Portland wheat is traded on the west coast of the United States.
  • It is correlated with Chicago wheat markets, but they are not the same
  • The differences in price movements are significant enough that traders on the west coast would be better served by using Portland wheat prices
  • Portland wheat prices are published daily by the USDA at http://www.ams.usda.gov/mnreports/jo_gr110.txt
  • This application pulls together the daily reports to form the basis for an interactive technical charting tool

Base graph

The base graph shows

  • the daily prices for a range selectable by the user
  • the exponential moving average for a between 1 and 36 weeks

Future expansion
